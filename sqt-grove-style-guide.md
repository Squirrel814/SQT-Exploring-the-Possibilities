## 1. Core Visual Language

**Style Base**  
Whimsical storybook illustration. Classic children’s book warmth (Beatrix Potter + modern folk-art sensibility) meets detailed natural observation. Not cute-cartoon; expressive, grounded, slightly magical.

**Palette Foundation (Global)**  
- Deep forest greens: `#2E5A44`, `#4CAF50`, `#2E7D32`  
- Soft creams & parchment: `#DFD4B5`, `#EFEBE9`, `#FFF9C4`  
- Rich earth & wood: `#8C6239`, `#5D4037`, `#8D6E63`  
- Accent emerald & growth: `#4AF626`, `#81C784`, `#A5D6A7`  
- Celebration gold: `#FFD54F`, `#FF8F00`  

Use the exact palettes from `sqt-themes.sample.json` for each holiday/event.

**Textures (always present)**  
Wood grain (highly detailed), soft moss, leaf veins, acorn caps (whole/cracked/sprouting), squirrel fur with individual hairs suggested, bark, pebble surfaces, subtle dew or dust motes.

**Recurring Motifs (the Grove Language)**
- **Acorns** — whole, cracked open, sprouting, in piles, as currency or talismans.
- **Leylines** — glowing threads, braided vines, root networks that connect everything.
- **Ratatoskr** — the messenger squirrel. Often shown mid-leap, carrying a scroll, acorn, or small bundle. Stylized but recognizable.
- **Moon phases** — subtly present even on non-lunar compositions (in bark patterns, water reflections, eye highlights).
- **Bridges & Paths** — pebbles, single stepping stones, vine bridges.
- **Burrows & Caches** — protective curved architecture, soft interiors, hidden entrances.

## 2. Full Motif Library (Holiday-Tied)

### Recurring Holidays
- **Whisperwake of the First Message** (Day 1): fresh sprouts, first light beams, single ratatoskr with scroll or glowing acorn, morning dew droplets.
- **The Discerned Hoard** (Days 4–5): carefully sorted acorn piles, twig balance scales, quiet inspection paw, selective light falling on chosen items only.
- **Leybridge Threading** (Day 7): individual pebbles, braided vines forming small arches, glowing leylines under water or in roots, patient line of stepping stones.
- **Burrowstill Knowing** (Days 13–14): moss-lined curved walls, single warm lantern or root-filtered light, closed-eye squirrel in contemplation, layered soft textures.
- **Chatterseed Scatter** (Day 17): flying seeds and acorn caps caught in wind, multiple small arcs of motion, joyful outward scattering gestures, bright breezy light.

### Major Events (Hero’s Journey — stronger, more ceremonial treatment)
- **Hoardkeeper’s Joyful Reckoning** (Lun 3, Day 19): abundant yet ordered golden acorn piles, celebratory scattering with motion blur on seeds, multiple squirrels in harmonious activity, radiant warm light.
- **The Shadow Trial** (Lun 6, Day 19): heavy mist, obscured root paths, single resilient squirrel with clever posture, high dramatic contrast, silver moonlight accents on fur and eyes only.
- **The Burrow Rebirth** (Lun 9, Day 19): breakthrough of soft new green light, delicate fresh shoots through old moss/bark, transformed or renewed posture, gentle hopeful atmosphere, old structures gently falling away at edges.
- **The Messenger’s Circuit Complete** (Lun 12, Day 19): full circular leyline mandala or integrated symbol, Ratatoskr at center carrying a woven glowing thread, every previous motif represented in harmonious balance, full warm spectrum with strong golden circuit lines.

**Rule:** Recurring = subtle accent within base palette. Majors = stronger color shift + more symbolic density + ceremonial_boost lighting.

## 4. Typography & Widget UI Patterns

- **Primary text / headings:** Space Grotesk or rounded sans (warm, friendly, slightly condensed).
- **Time / mono data:** Space Mono or system monospace. Emerald or cream on dark forest backgrounds.
- **Accents & highlights:** Subtle emerald glow (`#4AF626` with low opacity), soft golden rim lighting on important elements.
- **Backgrounds:** Deep forest `#0b0f12` or `#1E1E1E` with texture overlays (very subtle wood/moss grain).
- **Cards / modals:** Warm cream or parchment inner with thin emerald border. Rounded corners (12–16px).
- **Interactive states:** Gentle scale + glow on hover (Leybridge pebble feel). Never harsh.

**Accessibility**  
High contrast on all text. Prefer emerald or gold on dark, deep brown on cream. Avoid relying on color alone for meaning.

## 5. Prompt Guidelines for Generative Art & The Messenger’s Circuit

### Tiered Assembly (to protect SQT Grove Style)
Always assemble prompts in this order (see second-opinion integration in design_notes.md):

1. **System Base (Ratatoskr voice anchor)**  
   "You are Ratatoskr, the messenger squirrel of the SQT Grove. Speak with mythic yet grounded wisdom — playful but never silly, wise but never preachy."

2. **Holiday / Event Lexicon Block** (pull directly from `sqt-themes.sample.json`)  
   Include: palettes, motifs, style_modifiers, tone_keywords.

3. **Optional User / Project Context** (when Project-Deep or Curriculum mode).

4. **Structural Output Anchor** (strict 5-element bundle schema).

**Core Rule for all image/art prompts:**  
Always append: ", in the SQT Grove Style: whimsical storybook illustration, strong natural textures, warm forest palette"

**Mood Board Construction (text description for bundle):**
- List 3–4 specific colors by hex or name.
- Name 1–2 motifs.
- 1–2 sentence atmosphere.
- 2–3 concrete visual elements.
- One ready image prompt.

**Squirrel Protagonists:** Expressive faces and body language. Thoughtful, determined, joyful, resilient — never exaggerated cartoon.

## 6. Full Messenger’s Circuit Bundle Examples (3+)

These are complete, cohesive, ready-to-use 5-element examples generated according to the style guide and themes data. They demonstrate tone consistency and holiday flavoring. (MVP Prompt Template Library)

### Example 1 — Standard Mode, Leybridge Threading (Day 7)
**Journal Prompt**  
What small bridge are you threading today? One deliberate pebble, one quiet connection — what incremental link between people, ideas, or projects deserves your steady attention right now?

**Mood Board**  
Palette: deep forest green `#2E5A44`, soft cream `#DFD4B5`, warm brown `#8C6239`.  
Motifs: individual pebbles, braided vines, glowing leylines.  
Atmosphere: Patient golden-hour light over a gentle forest stream.  
Elements: A focused squirrel placing one smooth pebble at a time on a growing path; faint glowing lines visible beneath the water; highly detailed bark and wood grain.  
Image prompt: "A determined squirrel carefully placing smooth pebbles to form a small bridge across a forest stream, glowing leylines in the water, highly detailed wood grain, soft golden atmospheric dust, in the SQT Grove Style"

**Story Seed**  
One pebble at a time, the squirrel began laying a path across the rushing water. The current was strong, but each stone found its place with patience. By the time the sun touched the far bank, a narrow but certain bridge had appeared.

**Art Prompt**  
A determined squirrel carefully placing smooth pebbles to form a small bridge across a forest stream, glowing leylines in the water, highly detailed wood grain, soft golden atmospheric dust, in the SQT Grove Style: whimsical storybook illustration, strong natural textures, warm forest palette

**Foraging Idea**  
Today, choose one small connection to strengthen. Send the message, make the introduction, or add one linking note between two of your active projects. One pebble only.
